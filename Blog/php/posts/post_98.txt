<section class="article-container" data-stats-ve="35"><h1>Create a blog</h1><div class="inline-feedback__container"><div class="article-content-container"><div class="cc"><div class="no-margin"><p>You<span id="340130E"></span> can create and manage your own blog&nbsp;with Blogger.</p>
</div>

<h2>Create a blog</h2>

<p class="no-margin"><ol>
  <li><a href="https://www.blogger.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Sign in to Blogger</a>.</li>
  <li>On the left, click the Down arrow <img src="//lh3.googleusercontent.com/djaIkLnbwYMPGqs5ApyjzRDWdUL0Dx2Ih_hOcePjwqbwDUIzHztM2UY_E6L56hplYG3f=w36-h36" width="18" height="18" alt="Down arrow" title="Down arrow" data-mime-type="image/png" data-alt-src="//lh3.googleusercontent.com/djaIkLnbwYMPGqs5ApyjzRDWdUL0Dx2Ih_hOcePjwqbwDUIzHztM2UY_E6L56hplYG3f">.</li>
  <li>Click <span id="340128S"></span><strong>New blog</strong><span id="340128E"></span>.</li>
  <li>Enter a name for your blog.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Next</strong>.</li>
  <li>Choose a blog address&nbsp;or URL.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Save</strong>.</li>
</ol>
</p>

<div><p><strong>Note</strong>: Make sure you comply with the <a href="https://www.blogger.com/content.g" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Blogger Content Policy</a> and <a href="https://www.google.com/policies/terms/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Terms of Service</a>.</p>
</div>

<h2>Manage your blog</h2>

<div class="no-margin"><ul>
  <li>You can manage who can edit and read your blog. <a href="/blogger/answer/42673">Learn how to control access to your blog</a>.</li>
  <li>You can choose how you want your blog to look. <a href="/blogger/answer/176245">Learn how to change the design of your blog</a>.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<h3>Browsers you can use</h3>

<div><ul>
  <li>Chrome</li>
  <li>Firefox</li>
  <li>Safari</li>
  <li>MS Edge</li>
</ul>
</div>
